Project Overview

This project is one of the works I submitted as a Junior Designer for the Live Design Project. The Live Design Project is a program designed for interior design students to gain real-world experience by working on actual projects with real clients, helping them build their portfolios.

For this project, I collaborated with a client in Annecy, France, to redesign the family room of a hotel in this popular tourist destination.

Overview of the hotel room redesign in Annecy, France

Concept and Catalogue

As a holiday destination by Annecy Lake, the room's design draws inspiration from a cozy countryside ambiance. A country chalet to be exact, the room tries to emphasize a rustic and nature-inspired atmosphere, with a modern twist. The design prioritizes sustainability by incorporating eco-friendly products and reusing materials.

The layout of the hotel room is carefully designed to maximize the space while creating a comfortable and inviting atmosphere for guests. The design includes customized elements and thoughtful details that enhance the guest experience.
